Chicken Tacos

Description

These flavorful chicken tacos are quick, easy, and packed with authentic Mexican flavors. The chicken is seasoned with a perfect blend of spices and cooked until tender and juicy.

Ready in just 30 minutes, these tacos are perfect for busy weeknight dinners or casual entertaining. Serve with your favorite toppings and let everyone build their own perfect taco!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 12 corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cut into small bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add diced onion and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add chicken pieces to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes until nearly cooked through.
  6. Add chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, and cayenne pepper. Stir to coat the chicken.
  7. Pour in chicken broth and simmer for 2-3 minutes until chicken is fully cooked and liquid has mostly evaporated.
  8. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  9. Fill tortillas with chicken mixture and top with lettuce, tomatoes, red onion, cheese, sour cream, avocado, and cilantro.
  10. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side.
